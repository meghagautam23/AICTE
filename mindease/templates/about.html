Project Overview

MindEase is an AI emotional wellness companion designed for students who need structured, accessible, and stigma-free support during academic pressure.

Core Capabilities

  • Secure login/signup with personalized account space.
  • Mood tracking with sentiment analysis over journal notes.
  • AI chat companion with per-user chat history.
  • Emergency prompt when repeated negative patterns are detected.
  • Weekly dashboard and motivational quote generator.

Why It Matters

Students often need an always-available first line of emotional support. MindEase helps them observe mood patterns and encourages timely escalation to professional support when needed.
