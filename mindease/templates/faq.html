Is MindEase a replacement for therapy?

No. MindEase is a supportive wellness companion and does not replace professional diagnosis or treatment.

How does sentiment analysis work here?

The app uses a VADER sentiment model to score text and classify it as positive, neutral, or negative for lightweight emotional trend tracking.

What triggers emergency contact suggestions?

If repeated negative sentiment is detected across recent mood/chat entries, the app recommends immediate support contacts such as 988.

Is my data separated from other users?

Yes. Authentication is required and all mood logs and chat history are stored per user in SQLite.
