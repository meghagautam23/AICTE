Emergency Helplines (US)

  • 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline: Call or text 988 (24/7)
  • Emergency Services: 911
  • Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741

Student Mental Health Tips

  • Use a fixed sleep window for at least 5 nights a week.
  • Break study sessions into 25-30 minute focus blocks.
  • Talk to a friend, mentor, or counselor before stress escalates.
  • Track your emotions daily to detect early warning signs.
  • Keep hydration, movement, and nutrition consistent during exams.

Campus Support Recommendation

If distress persists for multiple days, schedule an appointment with your college counseling center. MindEase is a companion tool and not a substitute for licensed medical care.
